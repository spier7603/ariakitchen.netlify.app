##### The ingredients needed to make Beetroot Appe:

1. Make ready 1 tbsp Besan/ gram flour
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Beetroot pulp
1. Take 1/2 cup Curd
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Carrots  finely chopped
1. Get 1 tsp Green chilli
1. Take 1/4 tsp Adrak
1. Get to taste Salt
1. Get 1 tsp Eno
1. Get 1 tsp Oil
